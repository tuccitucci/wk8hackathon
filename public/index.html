How to Play

It's a super easy social drinking game where a random name, backstory and task to complete is generated on click. You can become anyone, even your Uncle. All you have to do is Sign-Up or Log-In and let the shenanigans begin. Have your friends join in for the fun, because the more the merrier. Let them score you on you tasks completed based on a simple YES, NO, MAYBE for the completion of the task.
